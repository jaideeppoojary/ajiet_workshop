AJ Institute of Engineering & Technology (AJIET) is a private engineering college located in Mangalore, Karnataka, India. Established in 2016, it is promoted by the Laxmi Memorial Education Trust, founded in 1991 in memory of Late Laxmi Shetty, mother of Dr. A. J. Shetty, the President and Managing Director of the Trust.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Doctoral Programs:

Ph.D. in various engineering disciplines
The college is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Infrastructure:

AJIET boasts modern facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities:

Classrooms and Laboratories: Well-equipped classrooms and specialized laboratories for various engineering disciplines.

Library: A comprehensive library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Seminar Hall: A spacious seminar hall for conferences, workshops, and cultural events.

Sports Facilities: Cricket ground, football ground, basketball court, volleyball court, and indoor games like table tennis, carrom, and chess.

Admissions:

Admission to undergraduate programs typically requires a valid score in the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) or equivalent examinations. For postgraduate programs, candidates must have a relevant undergraduate degree. Specific eligibility criteria and application procedures are available on the college's official website. 
AJIET

Placements:

AJIET has a dedicated Training & Placement Department that conducts various placement-related activities, including training sessions and placement drives. The department has a central placement team with staff representatives from every technical department overseeing its activities. Student coordinators act as an interface between the student group and staff placement coordinators.
