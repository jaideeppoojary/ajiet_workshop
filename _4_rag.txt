AJ Institute of Engineering and Technology (AJIET) in Mangalore is a premier educational institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. Established in 1991, AJIET has carved a niche for itself in the educational landscape of India, offering a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines.

### **Undergraduate Programs**

AJIET offers several undergraduate programs, primarily in the field of engineering. The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program is one of the most sought-after courses at the institution. The B.Tech program is offered in various specializations, including:

1. **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)**

   - **Fees:** Approximately INR 2,00,000 per year

2. **Mechanical Engineering (ME)**

Each of these specializations is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for successful careers in their respective fields.
